==============================================================
  FAQ — SUGGESTED Q&A (add to homepage or /faq page)
==============================================================

Q: Is my client's financial data kept confidential?
A: Yes. Uploaded bank statements are processed to generate
   your report and then permanently deleted within 24 hours.
   We never store, sell, or share transaction data.

Q: Do you use my documents to improve your system?
A: No. Your uploaded files and the data in them are never
   used to train, improve, or update any system. Each file
   is processed for your report only, then deleted.

Q: Who at StatementScrub can see my uploaded documents?
A: No one. Processing is fully automated. No employee
   reviews, accesses, or views uploaded bank statements.

Q: Is the connection secure?
A: Yes. All file uploads and report data are transmitted
   over an encrypted TLS connection. We follow industry-
   standard security practices.

Q: What banks do you support?
A: StatementScrub works with PDF statements from all major
   US banks including Chase, Bank of America, Wells Fargo,
   Citi, US Bank, and hundreds more.
